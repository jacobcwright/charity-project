What is Charity?

Definition of Charity

"Charity is the pure love of Christ. It is the love that Christ has for the children of men and that the children of men should have for one another. It is the highest, noblest, and strongest kind of love and the most joyous to the soul (see 1 Nephi 11:23)."

"Charity: An Example of the Believers" by lds.org

Ways to Practice Charity

  • Hold the door open for someone.
  • Do a chore for someone without them knowing.
  • Compliment a friend.
  • Help a friend study.
  • Donate to charity.
  • Smile!
  • Volunteer in service activities.
  • Call a loved one or friend.
  • A simple "thank you".
  • Minister in the moment.
  • Compliment your roommates.
